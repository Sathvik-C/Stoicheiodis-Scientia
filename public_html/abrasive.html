Abrasive Flow Finishing

Precision-engineered media for superior surface finishing, deburring, and polishing across high-precision industries

What is Abrasive Flow Finishing?

Abrasive Flow Machining (AFM) is an advanced precision finishing process that uses a semi-solid abrasive media to polish, deburr, and improve surface finish of complex internal and external geometries. Our specially formulated media flows through or across the workpiece, removing material in a controlled manner to achieve exceptional surface quality and dimensional accuracy.
