What is Therapy Putty?

Therapy putty is a specially formulated silicone-based exercise material designed for hand and finger rehabilitation, strength building, and fine motor skill development. Our therapeutic putty provides consistent resistance levels, making it ideal for occupational therapy, physical rehabilitation, and stress management. Non-toxic, non-oily, and safe for all ages, it's the perfect tool for recovering from injuries, improving grip strength, or simply relieving daily stress.

Exercise Guide

  • Full Grip Squeeze: Wrap putty in palm and squeeze repeatedly for overall hand strength
  • Finger Pinch: Pinch putty between thumb and each finger to improve pinch strength
  • Finger Extension: Wrap putty around fingers and spread to strengthen extensors
  • Wrist Rolls: Roll putty into a ball and rotate wrist for flexibility
  • Thumb Opposition: Press thumb to each fingertip through putty for coordination
  • Progressive Training: Start with softer resistance and advance as strength improves
